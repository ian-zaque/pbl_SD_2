Display LCD:
	RS: GPIO-25 (8 - 15 - 25)
	RW: GND
	E:  GPIO-01 (0 - 3 - 1 )
	D4: GPIO-12 (4 - 6 - 12 )
	D5: GPIO-16 (4 - 18 - 16)
	D6: GPIO-20 (8 - 0 - 20 )
	D7: GPIO-21 (8 - 3 - 21 )
